Q: What is the work from home (WFH) policy?
A: We offer a hybrid work model. Employees can work from home up to 3 days per week. Full-time remote work may be available for certain roles based on team requirements and manager approval.

Q: What are the office hours?
A: Core hours are 10 AM to 5 PM IST, Monday through Friday. We have flexible start times — you can begin anywhere between 8 AM and 11 AM as long as you complete your 8 hours.

Q: What is the interview process?
A: Our hiring process typically involves: (1) Resume screening, (2) Initial HR call (30 mins), (3) Technical round (60–90 mins), (4) Culture fit interview (30 mins), and (5) Final offer discussion. The entire process usually takes 1–2 weeks.

Q: How long does the recruitment process take?
A: From initial application to offer letter, our process typically takes 1–3 weeks depending on the role and candidate pipeline.

Q: What documents are required during onboarding?
A: You will need: Aadhaar card, PAN card, last 3 months salary slips, relieving letter from previous employer, and educational certificates (10th, 12th, graduation and above).

Q: What is the leave policy?
A: Employees get 18 annual leaves, 12 sick leaves, and 12 casual leaves per year. Maternity leave is 26 weeks and paternity leave is 5 days. All leaves are accrued monthly.

Q: Is there a probation period?
A: Yes, we have a 3-month probation period for most roles. During this time, you will receive regular feedback and a formal evaluation at the end of probation.

Q: What benefits and perks are offered?
A: We offer: health insurance (employee + family), annual performance bonus (10–30% of CTC), flexible work hours, meal allowance, internet reimbursement for remote work, learning & development budget (₹20,000/year), and company-sponsored certifications.

Q: How is performance reviewed?
A: Performance reviews are conducted twice a year (April and October). You will set OKRs with your manager at the start of each cycle and receive structured feedback at review time.

Q: What is the notice period?
A: Standard notice period is 30 days for junior roles and 60 days for senior/lead roles. This is negotiable in special circumstances.

Q: How do I apply for a job?
A: You can apply by sending your updated resume to hr@company.com or through our Telegram hiring channel. Please mention the role you are applying for in the subject line.

Q: What is the dress code?
A: We follow a smart casual dress code in the office. There is no strict formal requirement except for client meetings or external events.

Q: Do you provide relocation assistance?
A: Yes, for candidates relocating from another city, we provide a one-time relocation allowance of up to ₹50,000 depending on the distance and role level.

Q: What technologies and tools does the team use?
A: We use a modern tech stack including Python, React, AWS/GCP for infrastructure, Jira for project management, Slack for communication, and GitHub for version control.

Q: Is there a referral program?
A: Yes! Employees can refer candidates and earn a referral bonus of ₹10,000–₹50,000 (depending on role level) upon the referred candidate completing 3 months.
